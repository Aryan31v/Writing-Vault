# Substack Strategy

## Vision & Goals
*   **Generate Income:** How will this be achieved (e.g., paid subscriptions, sponsorships, affiliate marketing)?
*   **Build Community:** Strategies for engagement (e.g., comments, Q&A, Discord, polls).
*   **Share Expertise:** Focus on cognitive performance, personal development, productivity, learning faster, neuroplasticity.

## Niche & Target Audience
*   **Primary Topics:** Productivity, faster learning, neuroplasticity, avoiding "brain rot."
*   **Content Pillars:** Practical strategies, scientific insights, personal reflections.
*   **Target Audience Profile:** Who are they? What are their pain points, aspirations?

## Content Plan
*   **Format:** Personal reflections, how-to guides, summaries of literature.
*   **Cadence:** How often will new content be published?
*   **Content Ideas:**
    *   "My Daily Routine for Peak Productivity" (Personal Reflection/How-To)
    *   "Understanding Neuroplasticity: How Your Brain Can Learn Faster" (Literature/How-To)
    *   "Combatting Digital Brain Rot: Practical Steps" (How-To)
    *   "The Science of Focus: What I Learned from [X Study]" (Literature/Reflection)

## Marketing & Growth
*   **Launch Strategy:** How to get initial subscribers.
*   **Promotion:** Social media, cross-promotion, collaborations.
*   **SEO/Discovery:** Keywords, discoverability on Substack.

## Monetization Plan
*   **Free vs. Paid Content:** What content will be free, what will be behind a paywall?
*   **Pricing Structure:** Monthly, annual, founding member tiers.
